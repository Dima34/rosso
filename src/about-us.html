Our story

The POCCO company was created in january of 1998 as high quality furniture and components for furniture production shipper From the beginning, we made a rule to supply a product that would be required by furniture makers and be beneficial for them.

As a result, we have selected several well-known European brands with the best price for you. Among them: STABILUS, FRANKE, TEKA (Germany), METAKOR (Belgium), FGV, GIUSTI, BOSETTI-MARELLA, EKOTECH (Italy), GAMET, NOMET, REJS, KORNER (Poland), LINCOS (Russia), and others.

Every year, starts from 1999 we participate in the main furniture exhibition of the USA. Also we take a part in state exgibitions many times. Company employees regularly attend various furniture exhibitions.

Thanks to constant communication with customers from different regions and lost of exgibitions, we are able to stay informed of new furniture trends and buy the best items

Furniture handles

The company has changed over the years but we still stick to our principies: deliver only quality goods, choosing the one we like.

We have become specialists in one of the most complex and, at the same time, the most beautiful directions of furniture accessories - face hardware, or rather - furniture handles.

/1 Warehouse program of handles

POCCO was able to create a warehouse program of pens, very interesting for furniture designers, because in this collection you can see not only traditional pens of classical or modern style, in coating, for example, bronze or chrome, but also pens for the newest, fashionable directions of kitchen and other furniture, with unusual colors and textures of coatings:

/2 Professional assistance

It is no coincidence that most of the largest furniture factories in Russia have become our customers in recent years. This was achieved, among other things, because we and our foreign suppliers professionally help in the selection of handles and ensure the fastest possible supply of the required number of samples.

/3 Ergonimical design

On the other hand, our handles will fall to the liking and will serve furniture buyers for a long time due to the quality of the coatings and its beautiful, ergonomic design, in which every detail is thought out - for example - a comfortable grip, the absence of any inconvenient for the fingers stitches, sharp edges, voids, etc.; all handles are made for the joy of using the furniture.

In addition to pens, POCCO company offers a wide and interesting range of goods for both furniture production and for private persons.

For small furniture manufacturers we can also make interesting offers on our product. It will be possible to pick it up from our representatives in the regions, or, if there is no such, we will send the goods by transport company.
